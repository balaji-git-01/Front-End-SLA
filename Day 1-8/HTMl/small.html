Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is a prominent Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the Indian cricket team in limited-overs formats. He is widely admired for his elegant batting style and calm leadership. (He was born on 30 April 1987 in Nagpur, Maharashtra.)

Rohit is famously known as the “Hitman of Indian Cricket” because of his ability to score big centuries with ease. He is the only player to score three double centuries in One Day Internationals. (ODI double centuries are extremely rare in cricket.)
