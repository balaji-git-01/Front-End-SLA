Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer land the former captain of the India national cricket team in all formats of the game.

Facts about Rohit Sharma

  • He is the only player to score three double centuries in ODIs.
  • He became the captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats.
  • He holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs: 264 runs.
